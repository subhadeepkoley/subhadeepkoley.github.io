Subhadeep Koley

I am a Software Engineer, working on Digital Image Processing, Pattern Recognition, Multi & Hyperspectral imaging, Deep Learning, Medical Imaging, etc. at The MathWorks, Hyderabad, India.

As a part of my job, I develop image processing, computer vision, deep learning algorithms in the spatial and frequency domain. My role also includes investigating, analyzing, and shipping solutions to complex image processing, computer vision, & deep learning problems encountered by engineers and scientists.

Prior to that, I worked as a Graduate Engineer Trainee at Johnson Controls, Mumbai, India.

Research Interests

My research focus is broadly centered around Image Processing, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning. Specifically, I work on Cross-Modal Face Sketch-Photo Recognition, Image Enhancement, Multimedia Information Security, etc.
